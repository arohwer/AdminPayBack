WHAT IS THE

INNOVATION CENTER?

The Neumont Innovation Center for Entrepreneurship is the hub for business and technology projects in innovation in the Greater Salt Lake area. Located at the Neumont College of Computer Science campus in Salt Lake City, Utah, the center serves as a tool for developing entreprenuerial business ideas for current college students, as well as graduates. The Innovation Center seeks to help up-and-coming entrepreneurs develop their business ideas into a business model or prototype, and begin to strategize how to market that idea. The program also assists in helping students and graduates network with other businesses and experts in the area to further the development of their projects in innovation.

FOUNDER

JERRY PAY

The Neumont Innovation Center for Entrepreneurship is Jerry Pay's latest and greatest project in innovation. He has developed his passions for business and teaching into a program to support the development of student and graduate ideas in the form of an incubator for entreprenuerial success. Pay is the founder and current manager of the Neumont Innovation Center, as well as the co-founder of Tech-Knowledge Consulting and the president of Global Business strategies. He also worked as the chair of the Business Systems Technology Management degree program at Neumont College of Computer Science in Salt Lake City, Utah for 6 years, in addition to serving as the Vice President of Strategic Planning and Sales at Liberty Homes for 5 years, and as a National Service Manager at 3M for 21 years. Jerry Pay holds a Bachelor's of Science degree in Microbiology and Chemistry from Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, as well as a Master's of Business Administration in Business Technology Management from Western Governors University in Salt Lake City, Utah.
